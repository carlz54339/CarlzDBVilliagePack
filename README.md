# CarlzDBVilliagePack
Extra roles for the roguelike logic deduction game Demon Bluff.

## Instructions
You will have to play Demon Bluff Endless for the Roles to be added.

## Downloading the Mod
1. Download MelonLoader, and install MelonLoader for Demon Bluff
2. Insert CarlzVilliagePack.dll into the Mods folder

## Roles

### Detective (Good/Villager)
Pick three characters. Learn how many of each type there are.

### Mayor (Good/Outcast)
Always starts off as Good. Turns Evil if adjacent to an Evil.

### Therapist (Good/Villager)
Cures the Drunk if any are present. States who they cured.

### Painter (Good/Villager)
Gives two random pieces of information. One is true and the other is false. A Lying painter gives two false pieces of information.

### Germaphobe (Good/Villager)
Calls a Corruption source "dirty". Is immune to most corruptions, but not the Phantom's possession.

### Good Twin (Good/Outcast)
Disguises as a villager not in play. Always tells the truth. Creates an evil counterpart Disguising as the same role, who always lies. Causes loss of 8 HP when killed.

#### Evil Twin (Evil/Outcast)
Created by Good Twin in same role. If the Evil Twin is killed while the Good Twin remains alive, heal 5 HP.

### Executioner (Evil/Outcast)
Disguises as a Villager and chooses a good character as its target. If you execute the target, you lose. Always seen as truthful, but its information treats the target as the only Evil.

### Lycaon (Evil/Minion)
Temporarily kills a random Good character at the start of the game, causing a loss of 3hp. When the Lycaon is killed, the character it killed returns to life.

### Phantom (Evil/Demon)
Adds a random Outcast and Minion to the deck view. Instead of appearing in the village, the Phantom will Possess one Villager. The Possessed villager is Evil and Lying, and its abilities do not function.
